    Core Stat in Rolls¶

    When using a Core Stat directly in a roll (in which the Stat roll is not specified), choose 1 of the following: - Roll: - Roll 1d10 per 10 STAT(M) starting at 1d10. (if modifier is below 10, roll a 1d10) - Roll is the highest extra damage you can add. If roll is over stat, your limit is the stat #. - Auto-Roll: - Autoroll half the value of the Modifier (rounded down)
