    Weapons¶

    Note: For Weaponized combat write the weapon’s subclass. Can choose to roll die or use autoroll[auto] Assume Duration is Instant unless said otherwise

    Basic Info¶

    Strain¶

    • You can strain an item you are wielding by working it to its limits
    • When you use this mechanic, you must announce it before using/rolling for item
    • A character can do one or all options:
      • Increase weapon damage dealt (up to triple for 1 action: do damage roll X times)
      • Apply Damage Effect without needing to roll for it (up to 3 effects an action)
      • Apply Damage Effect multiple times (up to 3 Applications an action)
    • Roll a 1d20 for each damage increase and each damage effect.
      • Roll results are added to weapon, decreasing its durability
    • IMPORTANT:
      • You roll for item durability loss before you roll for accuracy
      • If you miss, durability loss still counts

    Other¶

    Natural Weapon - Claws, tusks and other natural add-ons are counted as weapons - They cannot be enchanted - Natural Weapons can use strain - Durability becomes HP Damage

    Undefined Weapon - An undefined weapon deals 1d20 (situation appropriate) damage and Has No Proficiency Bonus

    Enchantments - Ammo can be enchanted. Enchanting the weapon does nothing to the ammo. - Note: Blaster ammo cannot be enchanted

    Weapon Properties¶

    Side 1 Side 2
    Ammo:
    •   Used as ammo for weapon

    Ammunition:
    •   Must have ammunition for weapon to use properly

    Block:
    •   Action Points: Reaction (RP)
    •   Uses Material’s AMR to nullify incoming damage

    Charges:
    •   Uses per (defined time/object)

    ComboHit:
    •   Hits a target in multiple instances at the same time

    Cooldown:
    •   After use, # of AP before item can be used again

    Heavy:
    •   Increase damage by 1d20
    •   Increases Block by 5
    •   RE: [-15] ACC
    •   MovementSpeed:  -5 units

    Loading:
    •   Action, bonus action, or reaction to fire ammunition
    •   Ignores the # of attacks you can usually make    		 Magimmune:
    •   Anti-Myrr interferes with this weapon
       ◦   Prevents item from working
       ◦   AM damage to item deals Bonus 1d20 damage to item

    MultiHit:
    •   Hit a target multiple times

    MultiTarget:
    •   Targets multiple enemies at once

    Perforate:
    •   AOE
    •   Attack goes through all entities along path, applying damage (and effects)

    Prime:
    •   AP needed to prime weapon/ability before use

    Prime Power (PP):
    •   AP held in priming = more shots/damage

    Reach:
    •   Distanced melee attack
    •   Usually +1 unit in range

    Special:
    •   unusual rules, explain

    Thrown:
    •   If melee, use same modifier and damage roll

    Two-Handed:
    •   Must use 2 hands to use

    Categories¶

    Categories are the Family the weapon belongs into. Each category has several subcategories further defining the weapon.

    Weapons fit into at least one Category: - Bladed (8) - Blunt (4) - Pole (6) - Whip (3) - Bow (4) - Ammunition (4) - Shield (3) - Weird (6) - UMO (2) - Blaster (4)
