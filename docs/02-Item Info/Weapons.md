# Weapons
________________________________________
________________________________________

Note: For Weaponized combat write the weapon’s subclass.
Can choose to roll die or use autoroll\[auto]
Assume Duration is Instant unless said otherwise

## Basic Info
### Strain
-   You can strain an item you are wielding by working it to its limits
-   When you use this mechanic, you must announce it before using/rolling for item
-   A character can do one or all options:
	-   Increase weapon damage dealt (up to triple for 1 action: do damage roll X times)
	-   Apply Damage Effect without needing to roll for it (up to 3 effects an action)
	-   Apply Damage Effect multiple times (up to 3 Applications an action)
-   Roll a 1d20 for each damage increase and each damage effect.
	-   Roll results are added to weapon, decreasing its durability
-   IMPORTANT:
	-   You roll for item durability loss before you roll for accuracy
	-   If you miss, durability loss still counts

### Other
**Natural Weapon**
-   Claws, tusks and other natural add-ons are counted as weapons
-   They cannot be enchanted
-   Natural Weapons can use strain
	-    Durability becomes HP Damage

**Undefined Weapon**
-   An undefined weapon deals 1d20 (situation appropriate) damage and Has No Proficiency Bonus

**Enchantments**
-   Ammo can be enchanted. Enchanting the weapon does nothing to the ammo.
-   Note: Blaster ammo cannot be enchanted

## Categories
Categories are the Family the weapon belongs into. Each category has several subcategories further defining the weapon.

Weapons fit into at least one Category: 
-   Bladed (8)
-   Blunt (4)
-   Pole (6)
-   Whip (3)
-   Bow (4)
-   Ammunition (4)
-   Shield (3)
-   Weird (6)
-   UMO (2)
-   Blaster (4)

#### Miscellaneous
Enchantments don’t affect “shells”.

If a Blaster is Primed and is attacked/damaged, there is a 25% chance the weapon will explode (Splash damage based on energy left in Resource Chamber).

Primed Power (PP)
-   You can stretch out PP between multiple turns
-   PP has a Held Limit.
-   When you reach this limit, the gun fires automatically
