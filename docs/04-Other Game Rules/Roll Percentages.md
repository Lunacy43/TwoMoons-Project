### Coinflip
50% (XdX)= Choose:
-	Coinflip (choose one or another).  If chosen number, Success (auto-crit); if not, Fail (auto-fail).
-	An even sided die. Choose even or odd.  If chosen type, Success (auto-crit); if not, Fail (auto-fail).
