-	Duration:  -
-	Use: -
-	Resource: Free
-	Prerequisite: -
-	Description: 
	-	The entity has a growing craving to eat their own kind
	-	At the start of the day, the entity gains (6\[lv 1-6], 5\[lv 7-12], 4\[lv 13-18], 3\[lv 19-24], 2\[lv 25-30]) craving
